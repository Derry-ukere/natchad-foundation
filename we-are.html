Community Health Clinics

Natchard Foundation establishes community health clinics in underserved areas, offering free or low-cost medical services to those who cannot afford healthcare. These clinics provide primary care, health screenings, vaccinations, and health education programs, ensuring that individuals and families have access to essential healthcare services.
